About Me

I'm a high school student at Springdales School, Pusa Road, New Delhi (2014–2026), with a strong interest in interdisciplinary research, innovation, leadership, and community impact. My projects begin with a practical problem: pollution, waterlogging, student wellbeing, access to hygiene, or how young people engage with science.

Beyond the lab, I explore art, animation, vision boards, and creative work — believing the best ideas live at the intersection of disciplines. I was awarded the title of Mr. Springdales, the top all-round personality title at my school.
