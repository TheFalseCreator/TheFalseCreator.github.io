Health Technology / Humanistic Design

NEURO
HEALTH

An EXG sensor wearable concept that detects stress signatures in real time for high-stress students who lack timely mental-health support.

Year

Grade 11

Domain

Health Tech

Award

INR 20,000, Humanistic Design Summit 2

Status

Design brief

Mental health support before the crisis point.

High-stress students often lack timely mental health support. NeuroHealth proposes a wearable system using EXG signals to detect stress signatures and create an alert pathway before stress becomes invisible damage.

The Problem

Students may experience stress without early support or visible warning signs.

The Process

Study signals, detect patterns, define alerts, and design a dashboard.

The People I Met

Design summit evaluators pushed the project toward human-centered clarity.

The Outcome

INR 20,000 award at Humanistic Design Summit 2, Pan India.

Components / Materials Used

The health-tech system.

EXG Sensors

Capture physiological signals connected to stress states.

Signal Processing

Filters and interprets stress-related signatures.

Alert System

Creates a path from detection to timely support.

Data Dashboard

Visualizes trends with privacy and care in mind.

Key Outcomes

Humanistic design, not gadget-first design.

  1. Identified a high-stakes student wellbeing problem.
  2. Converted a sensor idea into a care pathway.
  3. Won INR 20,000 for excellence in problem identification.
