Sustainability / Materials Science

GREEN DESIGNING
OF BIOMASS

Biodegradable packaging from agricultural waste, designed to reduce air pollution and create a better economic alternative for farmers.

Year

Domain

Sustainability

Award

Status

Prototype / research expansion

The Problem → The Process → The People → The Outcome

The project began with stubble-burning air pollution and the question of whether agricultural waste could become a useful material instead of a hazard. I explored biodegradable packaging from biomass and later investigated a conductive composite by combining pollution-derived particulates with biomass substrate.

The Problem

Agricultural residue is often treated as waste, but it carries material value.

The Process

Collect, bind, form, test, present, and revise the packaging concept.

The Outcome

1st position at the Dr. A.K. Saha State Competition and a stronger sustainability research direction.
